Mathematical Study Portal

Minimalist LaTeX-styled math representations for the Jackrong LLM Fine-tuning Guide.

Available Guides

  • Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) Math
    Autoregressive sequence factorization, Softmax derivation, log-likelihood, and gradient backpropagation updates. Includes an interactive logit-to-gradient simulation.
  • SFT Weight Updates & First Principles
    Understanding how gradients on logits geometrically and mathematically update the un-embedding matrix, and how backpropagation routes signals through the entire neural network.
  • The Mathematics of LoRA & QLoRA
    A deep dive into how intrinsic dimension, matrix factorization, and NF4 quantization solve the VRAM crisis.
  • The Mathematics of GRPO
    Group Relative Policy Optimization — from why PPO's critic is a bottleneck to how DeepSeek eliminated it, with a fully worked dry run. Includes group advantage estimation, KL unbiased estimator, asymmetric clipping, and per-token loss computation with concrete numbers.
